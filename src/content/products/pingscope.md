PingScope gives you real-time visibility into what's happening with your network, not just "something's slow" but whether the failure looks local, upstream, or destination-side.

## Features

- **Menu bar native** — always-on latency visibility without keeping a dashboard open
- **Failure context** — monitor gateway and remote hosts together to separate Wi-Fi, router, ISP, and destination trouble
- **Pinned overlay** — keep a compact graph on screen during calls, uploads, and debugging sessions
- **Local first** — settings, history, exports, and widget snapshots stay on your Mac
